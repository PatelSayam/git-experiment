1. Wash and soak rice
2. Heat ghee in a pan
3. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle
4. Add onions and sauté until they turn golden brown
5. Add ginger and garlic paste and sauté for a minute
6. Add the soaked rice and mix well
7. Add water and salt to taste
8. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to low
9. Cover the pan with a lid and let it cook until the rice is cooked and fluffy
10. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot
